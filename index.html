Welcome to
Premchand Jute & Industries Pvt. Ltd.

Exporter and manufacturer of jute bags, hessian bags, jute sacks, jute yarn, hessian cloths, food grade jute bags for packing of cocoa beans, Coffee beans, Cashew nuts, Food grains and other agriculture produce. Since inception, Premchand Jute & Industries Pvt. Ltd. (PJIPL) has been the frontrunner in the Indian jute industry with innovative products, a skilled workforce, and pioneering management. Equipped with state-of-the art machineries at all stages, the company aims to become a leader in the industry and revolutionize how the industry is perceived in the world.

Our Mission

To produce top quality jute products creating value for customers and communities where we live and work.

Our Vision

To promote jute as an unmatched eco-friendly packaging solution to the world at large.

Switch to Jute - A natural choice

With increased global awareness about using environment friendly products, many countries are switching to the greener alternative in jute products, to meet its packaging requirements.
