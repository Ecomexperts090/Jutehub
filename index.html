Our Legacy

Driving the Green Revolution in Textiles

Global Jute was established with a singular vision: to replace synthetic materials with high-performance natural fibers. Today, we operate one of the most advanced jute composite plants in the region.

Our facility integrates state-of-the-art European machinery with traditional craftsmanship, allowing us to produce everything from raw yarn to intricate finished lifestyle products.
